NP Membership

Non-Profit Membership Benefits

  • Chamber membership listing in the online directory
  • Access to networking events at no extra charge
  • Monthly business newsletter subscription
  • Participation in community outreach programmes
  • Access to Chamber's meeting rooms (booking required)
  • Collaboration opportunities with corporate members

Annual Fee: No fee — open to registered non-profit organisations in the Grand Duchy.

Bronze Membership

Bronze Membership Benefits

  • Full directory listing with logo and contact details
  • Invitation to all Chamber networking events
  • 10% discount on Chamber-organised training courses
  • Monthly newsletter and weekly business digest
  • Access to Chamber's legal and regulatory helpdesk
  • One free seat at the Annual Business Forum
  • Business card exchange at quarterly mixers

Annual Fee: Contact the Chamber for current pricing.

Silver Membership

Silver Membership Benefits

  • All Bronze benefits included
  • Featured listing on Chamber homepage (rotating spotlight)
  • 20% discount on training programmes and workshops
  • Priority invitations to exclusive finance and policy roundtables
  • Logo placement in Chamber publications and reports
  • Two free seats at the Annual Business Forum
  • Access to member-only market intelligence reports
  • Dedicated Chamber liaison officer

Annual Fee: Contact the Chamber for current pricing.

Gold Membership

Gold Membership Benefits

  • All Silver benefits included
  • Permanent spotlight on Chamber homepage
  • Co-branding on Chamber event materials and banners
  • 30% discount on all training, events, and workshops
  • Complimentary table (8 seats) at the Annual Gala Dinner
  • Four free seats at the Annual Business Forum
  • Inclusion in all press releases and Chamber media
  • Priority speaking slots at Chamber events
  • Direct access to Chamber lobbying and policy team
  • Exclusive invitations to government receptions and EU briefings

Annual Fee: Contact the Chamber for current pricing.
