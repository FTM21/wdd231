Discover Luxembourg

A thriving economic powerhouse blended with thousands of years of historical European culture.

Region Demographics

  • Population: ~660,000
  • Official Languages: Luxembourgish, French, German
  • Capital: Luxembourg City
  • GDP per Capita: Highest in the EU

Why Invest Here?

Positioned neatly in the heart of Europe, Luxembourg offers unparalleled financial stability, an international open workforce, and a phenomenal quality of living for families and growing companies alike.
