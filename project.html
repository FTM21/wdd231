Urban Threads

Streetwear Clothing and Accessories Online Store

Site Purpose

The site provides a hub for streetwear enthusiasts, offering a wide range of authentic streetwear clothing and accessories. It includes detailed product descriptions, a smooth shopping experience, and style guides to help customers express their urban style.

Scenarios

  • What are the latest streetwear trends available?
  • How can I track my order after making a purchase?

Color Schema

Primary Color: Black (#000000)

Used for text, headers, and borders.

Secondary Color: White (#FFFFFF)

Used for background and sections to maintain contrast and readability.

Typography

Headings: Oswald, sans-serif

Used for all main headings to create a strong and modern look.

Body: Roboto, sans-serif

Used for body text to ensure clean readability.
